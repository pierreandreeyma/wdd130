Overview

Purpose

Unleash the Untamed Spirit of Haiti: Our purpose is to help you discover the Thrill of Cap-Haïtien's First Whitewater Rafting Adventure Cap-Haïtien, nestled in the heart of Haiti's breathtaking North Coast, has long whispered tales of adventure. We, at White Water Rafting, are here to turn those whispers into a roaring symphony of adrenaline and exhilaration. We're not just Haiti's first water rafting outfit; we're your gateway to experiencing the untamed spirit of this vibrant nation in a way never before possible.

Audience

European and North American: Age range 16-55, active lifestyle, adventurous spirit, disposable income, interest in outdoor activities and ecotourism, for nature lovers, thrill seekers, cultural explorers, foodies, off-the-beaten-path travelers, responsible tourism advocates.

Haitian: Age range 16-55, families with young children, groups of friends, young professionals, interest in local adventure and cultural experiences for family outings, group activities, team building experiences, discovering new parts of their own country, connecting with nature and local culture.

My audience will usually access my site via laptop device and mobile device.
